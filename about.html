About Us

About Our Company

Hiroek is a simple and intuitive digital nft platform which focuses on empowering creators, collectors and curators through blockchain NFT technology. Nft via blockchain technology will become the leading emerging asset class of the new virtual economy. We are expanding the art audience, offering a global reach and creating the future of art.

How do we create value

A platform designed for creators by creators We offer an extremely easy and convenient way for artists to express their creation and for collectors to share in the creation. People who can't attend a gallery in person can now easily collect authenticated and curated digitised art pieces from the comfort of their phone, tablet or laptop from anywhere in the world.

How we benefit a business.

We provide a gateway for exciting and innovative business to enter the nft space by future proving how business is done going forward. E.g. tattoo artists will benefit from being able to turn their work into nft. It allows artists to supply their clients with digital tattoos before they ever get a work done on their body. Hence an added source of revenue for them. For collectors they have access to a wider choice of design. They can now conveniently buy a tattoo from an artist in another country but get the work done locally.

What make us different

Our model creates an environment where nft meets charity; to strengthen the bond which has forever existed between art and philantropy. Minimum 10% of every transaction goes towards a partnering charity of the creators choice.
